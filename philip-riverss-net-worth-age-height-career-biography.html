Philip Rivers Biography

Philip Rivers was born on December 8, 1981, in Decatur, Alabama, but grew up in Athens, Alabama. He played high school football at Athens High School, where he was a standout quarterback. After graduating in 2000, Rivers attended North Carolina State University, where he played college football for four years.

In 2004, Rivers was selected by the San Diego Chargers with the fourth overall pick in the NFL Draft. He played for the Chargers for 16 seasons, earning eight Pro Bowl selections and leading the team to the playoffs six times. In 2020, Rivers signed with the Indianapolis Colts for his final NFL season, where he helped lead the team to the playoffs.
