Sophie Turner Wears the Cutest Overalls in NYC with Joe Jonas

  • Today, Sophie Turner and Joe Jonas were spotted strolling the streets of New York City after having a brief shopping spree at DITA and REI.
  • Turner sported a pair of blue denim overalls, a white shirt, white trainers, and a pair of dark shades.
  • The couple’s appearance precedes the MTV Video Music Awards, for which the Jonas Brothers will perform tonight.

Overall, we know we can count on Game of Thrones darling Sophie Turner to up the ante when it comes to sartorial decision-making. From boldly taking to the New York City streets in socks and sandals to wearing the miniest of minidresses in the cutest gingham number, Turner is on the rise to becoming one of television’s favorite style icons. And today, the actress flaunted her latest trendsetting fit: a pair of denim overalls.

Accompanied by husband Joe Jonas, the Emmy-nominated actress gave the farmer girl look a chic twist, pairing Mango's Flared Denim Dungarees with a long-sleeved white tee, white trainers, and dark shades. The look is a cool slide from summer miniskirts into fall bell bottoms, an indicator your wardrobe's transition into cooler weather is imminent.
