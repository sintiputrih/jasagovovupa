When it comes to collabs, there’s nothing we love more than two of our favorite brands joining forces to create something that completely blows us away. So when we heard that HOKA partnered up with Free People’s FP Movement line to create a collection of four limited edition sneakers, we couldn’t help but get excited. After all, you’re bound to get the podiatrist-approved comfort and support that you’d expect from a HOKA shoe with the cool, boho-chic vibe of Free People. Sounds like a win-win to us! Best part? You can shop the collection right now.

The new HOKA x FP Movement Collection features the Kawana 2, the Clifton L, and the oh-so popular Clifton 9 in limited edition colorways and patterns. The two iconic brands actually teamed up once before back in 2022 and was super successful (naturally). According to a press release, the new April 2024 collection was inspired by both brand’s shared goal of empowering the adventurous spirit within all of us. So, you’re sure to find brand new colorways that really speak to that.

As fans of both brands, we can confidently say that this new collection does not disappoint. The new Clifton 9 options (there are two!) feature the dreamiest colors, while the Clifton L suede is very Free People. Given the popularity of both brands, we highly doubt these will stay in stock for long. They’re available at HOKA and Free People. So, be sure to snag a pair before they sell out.

Clifton 9 FPM in Cirrus

HOKA’s Clifton 9 is a popular style among shoppers for being lightweight, breathable, and super, super comfortable. You can pretty much wear these all day long without needing to take them off. In fact, numerous shoppers have said these feel like walking on clouds. An earlier variation of the style has even been spotted on celebs like Cameron Diaz. This new Cirrus shade from the HOKA x FP Movement collection features pretty watercolor-like shades that are perfect for spring.

Clifton 9 FPM in Eggnog

If you’d rather have a shoe that’s a little more versatile in color (though you can totally sport the above with anything you want), consider the Clifton 9 in eggnog. We adore the light purple and blue accents, they’re so cute. Plus, you get the same great features as all the Clifton 9s including the compression molded EVA midsole for durability and comfort.

Kawana 2 FPM in White

The HOKA Kawana 2 features a single-layer mesh upper for breathability and a thin, sock-like internal bootie that perfectly fits around your feet, keeping them in place. These are great for running on all surfaces, and feature HOKA’s infamous support and comfort as well. Not only that, it looks absolutely gorgeous.

Clifton L Suede FPM in Ocean

We are loving the retro look and feel of the Clifton L Suede from the HOKA x FP Movement collection. These were designed to be ultra lightweight and feature a “cushy, compression molded” insole. While it’s hard to pick just one favorite from the new drop, this one’s definitely one of our faves.
