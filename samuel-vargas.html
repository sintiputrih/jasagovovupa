Samuel Vargas - Bio, Age, Net Worth, Height, Married, Facts

Table of Biography

Samuel Andres Vargas Ariza, better known as Samuel Vargas, is a professional boxer from Colombia who competes in the welterweight/light middleweight division. Since turning professional in 2010, Samuel Vargas has had a 31-7-2 record (win-loss-draw).

Early Lifestyle and Childhood

Samuel Vargas, a Colombian native, was born on April 12th, 1989, under the sign of Aries. The thirty-four-year-old has dual Colombian and Canadian citizenship.

Samuel Vargas – Professional Life

He made his professional debut at Todd Furler on February 6, 2010. The fight ended in a TKO victory for him. In a fight against Manolis Platis, he captured his first professional title, the NCC welterweight title, after eight fights. He went on to win two more fights before winning his second championship, the WBA-NABA Canada super welterweight title, against Julius Bunda on April 9, 2013.

Similarly, He defeated Robson Assis for the WBA-NABA interim welterweight championship on December 11, 2015, at The Mattamy Events Center in Toronto, Ontario, Canada. He captured the WBA-NABA welterweight championship by unanimous decision over Juan Armando Garcia on May 14, 2016. Likewise, He fought Conor Benn for the WBA Continental welterweight championship on April 10, 2021. He was knocked out in the first round by TKO.
