Miles Bridges is one of the most famous American professional basketball players. Not only this, Miles Bridges is better known as the player for the Charlotte Hornets of the National Basketball Association (NBA). Interestingly, let us know more about the player and his professional as well as personal life.

Early Life

The player, Bridges was born on March 21, 1998. As of 2024, he is 25 years old. Similarly, his birthplace is in Flint, Michigan, United States. Miles belongs to American nationality whereas his ethnicity is inaccessible. Also, his parents are Cynthia and Raymond Bridges. Raymond Bridges was a two-time basketball state champion at Flint Northern High School.

Caption: Miles Bridges with his father (Source: Instagram)

Additionally, Miles also has a sister as a sibling, Tara Rushing. Talking about his education, he and his sister attended middle school at Woodland Park Academy in Grand Blanc, Michigan. When Miles was 12, he used to practice at a local YMCA with future high school teammates and coach Keith Gray.

Later on, Miles joined Huntington Prep School in Huntington, West Virginia.

Miles Bridges – Career

Initially, Bridges played varsity basketball with Flint Southwestern Academy as a fresher.  In 2015, he and Huntington Prep played in Dick’s Sporting Goods High School Nationals Tournament. During the same year, he also participated in the NBPA Top 100 Camp at the John Paul Jones Arena.

Talking about his college career, he declined offers from schools such as Kentucky, Kansas, and Oregon. In 2015, he decided to play for Michigan State. During his college career, he was able to receive various kinds of titles as well as appreciation.

Regarding his professional career, Bridges was finally selected with the twelfth overall pick by the Los Angeles Clippers in the 2018 NBA draft. Subsequently, he traded to the Charlotte Hornets in exchange for the pick before him, Shai Gilgeous-Alexander. Currently, he has been playing for Charlette Hornets since the year 2018.
