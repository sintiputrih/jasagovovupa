Kieran Culkin is an American actor, stage artist, and voice actor who started his career as a child artist. He is known for having been cast in movies like Father of the Bride, Igby Goes Down, Home Alone, Home Alone 2: Lost in New York, Movie 43, Father of the Bride Part II, The Mighty, The Cider House Rules, The Magical Legend of the Leprechauns, The Dangerous Lives of Altar Boys, Scott Pilgrim vs. the World, Wiener-Dog, Paper Man, and Infinity Baby. In addition to that, his television roles include playing Roman Roy in the HBO satirical black comedy-drama series Succession, Andy “Fish” Troutner in the NBC sitcom Go Fish, Peter (voice) in the animated sitcom Long Live the Royals, and Joe Jonas/Nostradamus’s Intern (voice) in the adult animated stop motion sketch comedy series Robot Chicken.

    Kieran Culkin Facts

  • His parents separated in September 1995 and he has been estranged from his father.
  • Kieran Culkin made his first theatrical film appearance by playing the character of Fuller McCallister, Kevin’s youngest cousin, in the 1990 comedy film Home Alone, the first film in the Home Alone franchise.
  • He was cast as Matty Banks in the 1991 comedy film Father of the Bride and this performance got him nominated for a Young Artist Award for “Best Young Actor Co-starring in a Motion Picture”.
  • Alongside Claire Danes, Jeff Goldblum, Amanda Peet, Ryan Phillippe, Bill Pullman, and Susan Sarandon, Kieran Culkin co-starred as Jason “Igby” Slocumb, Jr., a rebellious and sardonic teenager, in the 2002 comedy-drama film Igby Goes Down. This role won him the Broadcast Film Critics Association Award for “Best Young Performer” and Satellite Award for “Best Actor – Motion Picture Musical or Comedy” while he was also nominated for a Golden Globe Award for “Best Actor – Motion Picture Musical or Comedy”.
  • In March 2022, Kieran won the “Best Supporting Actor in a Drama” award at the Critics Choice Awards for his role in Succession.
  • During the 2024 Golden Globe Awards, Kieran received the “Best Actor” award for his work in Succession.
  • In January 2024, he won the “Best Actor in a Drama Series” award at the Critics Choice Awards for his work in Succession. For the same role, he won the Primetime Emmy Award for “Outstanding Lead Actor in a Drama Series” in January 2024.
