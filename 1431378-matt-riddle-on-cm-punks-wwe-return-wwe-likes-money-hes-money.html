Matt Riddle On CM Punks WWE Return: WWE Likes Money, Hes Money

Regarding CM Punk‘s return to WWE, Matt Riddle says you can’t argue with a man that is money.

WWE released former United States Champion Matt Riddle in September. The company has changed a lot since his departure, as CM Punk made his buzzworthy return at WWE Survivor Series.

Speaking with MMA Junkie, Matt Riddle shared his thoughts on Punk’s return to WWE.

“I’m not surprised because WWE, they like money and CM Punk is money, just like he was money in Mixed Martial Arts,” Matt Riddle said. “You can’t argue with a man that is money. That’s like with UFC. I know if I fight, I’ll sell tickets. It’s just if they want it
