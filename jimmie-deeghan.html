How did Jimmie Deeghan start his Professional Career?

Deeghan joined the pop-punk band Every Avenue in 2007 as rhythm guitarist and backup vocalist. He was a part of the band’s studio albums Shh, Just Go With It (2008), Picture Perfect (2009), and Bad Habits (2011), which featured the songs Where Were You, Saying Goodbye, Fall Apart, Tie Me Down, and Watch the World. The band paused in 2012 before continuing their journey in 2018.

Furthermore, the American musician has performed on multiple times with Ryan Hurd and Kane Brown, including the 2019 Lifeforever Tour, Ride All Night Tour, and Worldwide Beautiful Tour. He shared the stage alongside Brown at the Tortuga Music Festival, Stagecoach Festival, and CMA Fest + CMT Awards in 2019.

Jimmie became a full-time songwriter after signing with MV2 Entertainment in February 2018; other artists signed with the company include Thomas Archer, Clay Mills, and Terry McBride. Among the songs he’s written are One Thing Right, Lie To Me, and Never Seen The World.
