Early Life & Education

Trisha Paytas was born on May 8th, 1988, in Riverside, California, United States. She was born to Lenna Paytas (mother) and Frank Paytas (father).

She has two siblings, a younger maternal half-sister and an older brother. She spent most of her childhood with her parents and siblings in California. She has two siblings: Kalli Metz and Nick Paytas.

When Trisha Paytas was 15 years old, she moved back to California to be with her father and brother. She was then enrolled in a Catholic online school program and graduated from it.
