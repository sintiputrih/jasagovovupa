TMZ: Madonna Reportedly Getting Serious about 25-Year-Old Boyfriend Ahlamalik Williams, According to

Madonna has reportedly stepped things up between her and her 25-year-old boyfriend. The holidays may have speeded up the process.

According to TMZ, Madonna and her beau, Ahlamalik Williams, enjoyed a family-focused holiday that reportedly saw the legendary singer meet Williams' folks.

Allegations that the pair have been dating went on for months before Williams' father reportedly told TMZ they were definitely together. They first met after one of Madonna's shows.

Madonna's boyfriend Williams

Williams was a backup dancer for the star during a performance at Ceasar's Palace. When they finished, the celebrity invited the dancers to her hotel suite, where a chef cooked dinner for the group.

Before that, the "Like A Prayer" singer reportedly hand-selected Williams after his audition for her Rebel Heart Tour back in 2015. The rumors of them dating began in July 2018.

His father's support

TMZ further alleged that Williams' dad is happy for his son. The entire family is enjoying some perks as Madonna invited them to her London and France shows set for the start of 2020 — Yes, they're reportedly going.

Mirror also alleged that Williams' father is all for their relationship, even suggesting that the pair "could get married" in the future. He added that they are "consenting adults."

According to the outlet, Williams, a naval veteran, is thrilled to be with Madonna. The singer intends to take care of the talented young man, who is also a songwriter.

Traveling with family

Before she allegedly met the family, Madonna and her boyfriend were last spotted at the John F Kennedy airport with three of her children, including the oldest, Lourdes Leon, who is two years Williams' junior.

Twins Estere and Stelle were also with the pair. Madonna tried to go low-key through the airport, donning a black hoodie and coat. Williams also sported black as they made their way to the flight.

Madonna cancels shows

Back in November, Madonna was compelled to cancel three shows slated to run in Boston, Massachusetts in early December. The singer cited "overwhelming" pain as the reason.
