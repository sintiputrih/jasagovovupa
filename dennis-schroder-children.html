Dennis Schröder biography.

Dennis Schröder is a professional German Basketball player in the NBA for Los Angeles Lakers. He used to play in Germany for SG Braunschweig and Phantoms Braunschweig before getting drafted into the NBA.

Dennis was picked by the Atlanta Hawks in the first round of the 2013 NBA draft. He spent 5 years with the Hawks and was a prominent member of the team helping them reach the playoffs three times.

Later on in 2018, he was traded to the Oklahoma City Thunder. His time there was short but he put on impressive and amazing form until he parted ways with the team.

Dennis Schröder is currently having an extraordinary career at Los Angeles Lakers after being traded to the team in 2020 by the Thunder. His amazing and incredible performances helped the team win the NBA Championship in 2020.

Who are Dennis Schröder children?

Dennis Schröder and his wife, Ellen Zioho, have been happily married and they have welcomed 3 children into the world together.

They have been blessed with 2 boys and a girl. The names of their children are Dennis Jr., Imalia Aaliyah, and Awa.
