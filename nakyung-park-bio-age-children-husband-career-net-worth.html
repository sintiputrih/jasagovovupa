Nakyung Park Bio: Early Life and Career

Nakyung Park was born on 1 September 1977. She is 45 years in 2022. She was born in Seoul, South Korea. Her father, Park Cheol is a well-celebrated entertainment executive. The father has worked on several TV Shows as a director.

From Childhood, Park has a special desire to become an artist and a painter. With that burning desire, she finally pursued Graphic Design and Makeup Artistry.
