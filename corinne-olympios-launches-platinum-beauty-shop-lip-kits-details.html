Corinne Olympios Launches Platinum Beauty Shop Lip Kits: Details

Corinne Olympios didn’t receive Nick Viall’s final rose on his season of The Bachelor, so the blonde beauty went ahead and created her own final rose — in lipstick form. Earlier this year, Olympios launched her very own beauty brand, Platinum Beauty Shop (a cheeky nod to that infamous comment about her “platinum vagine”), which includes lipsticks, glosses and kits that are longwearing enough to get you through Thanksgiving dinner.

Kicking off the line, the former reality star debuted her Platinum Lipsticks — a collection of highly pigmented, long-lasting liquid matte formulas. The four shade offerings are pinky neutrals inspired by Olympios’ desire to create what she calls a “not your lip color, lip color.”

First, there is a rosy pink named Corn named after hers truly. There is also a beige-tinged hue called Trench Coat, in addition to two Bachelor-inspired shades: Final Rose (a dusty mauve) and Bounce House (a sparkly berry).

Taking a page from the Kardashian-Jenner playbook, the $29 Signature Lip Kits include a Platinum Liquid Matte Lipstick and the brand’s Platinum Prime Liner. Rather than match the lipstick exactly, Olympios’ liner is a colorless pencil that smooths lips, prevents feathering and helps to keep the color in place.

To add a glossy and pillowy finish to any of the liquid lips, Olympios created her $19 Balloon Lip Gloss Plumper. Worn alone or over any lipstick, the vanilla-scented, high-shine formula has a plumping effect for a super perky pout.

All the products in Platinum Beauty Shop are vegan and cruelty free (some are even kosher) and available exclusively at PlatinumBeautyShop.com.
