Maureen McCormick Biography; Net Worth, Age, Children And Husband

Maureen McCormick is an American actress, singer, and author. She is famous for her role as Marcia Brady in the sitcom “The Brady Bunch” which aired from 1969 to 1974. Maureen McCormick was born on 5 August 1956 in Los Angeles, California in the U.S.

Maureen McCormick is of Irish and German descent and was raised in a Catholic home. She was born to Irene Beckman and Richard McCormick into a family of three. Maureen McCormick started her career when she was at the age of six years. She won the Baby Miss San Fernando Valey beauty pageant at the age of six and appeared on national U.S television in 1964. Maureen McCormick appeared in movies such as “Bewitched” and “My three sons” in her early ages in the 1960s.

Maureen McCormick rose to fame between the years, 1969 and 1974 when she played the role of Marcia Brady in the sitcom “The Brady Bunch which aired from 1969 to 1974 on American Broadcasting company (ABC). Maureen McCormick has since gained more fame and recognition across the world and she was called by many to feature in their movies. In 2015, Maureen McCormick was called to play a role in the Australian version of Celebrity…Get Me Out of Here! She was the last to be evicted before the finale.
