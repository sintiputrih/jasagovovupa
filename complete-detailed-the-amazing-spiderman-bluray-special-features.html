Those features sound pretty epic to me. Personally, I’m really excited for the casting bit considering the casting of a reboot will always be compared to the previous version, adding another difficult dimension to an already heavily scrutinized process.

The title of the deleted scenes section: “A Different Fate,” instantly caught my attention too. Is that just a creative name for the section or does that ominous title possibly refer to scenes that complete the rumored “Untold Story” that the film ultimately avoided addressing?

Either way, this Blu-Ray sounds awesome, and I’ll definitely be putting in my pre-order tonight.

The Amazing Spider-Man Blu-Ray will hit shelves November 9th.
