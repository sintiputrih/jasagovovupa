Chloe Pacey - Age, Family, Bio

Life partner of actor Brenton Thwaites.

Before Fame

She met Brenton while he was filming the movie Pirates of the Caribbean: Dead Men Tell No Tales.

Trivia

Her chloepacey Instagram account had more than 10,000 followers prior to being deactivated. 

Family Life

She and Brenton have children together named Birdie and Pippa.

Associated With

Her partner Brenton starred opposite David Strathairn in the Christian film An Interview with God.
