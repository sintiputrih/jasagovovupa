Who Has A Higher Net Worth?

While Nicki Minaj is considered one of the most influential rap artists, Rihanna is one of the most jaw-dropping beauties and singers of our time. Both artists are talented, unique, charismatic, and pretty famous, but who has a higher net worth? To find out, let's take a look at their successful careers.

Nicki Minaj's Music Career

Nicki Minaj is an exceptional rapper, singer, songwriter, television personality, and actress known for her flowing quick-spoken rap. She complements her music with her bold persona, which consists of colorful wigs and outrageous outfits.

As one of the most influential female rap artists of all time, Nicki first entered the music business as a backup singer for local aspiring rap singers in New York City. She would upload her videos on her social media pages, and this is how she eventually came into attention and was discovered by the fellow American rapper Lil Wayne.

Eventually, the hip-hop artist headed to fame with her lit tracks like Super Bass, Starships, and Anaconda. Nicki has become the first female solo artist to have seven singles simultaneously on the Billboard Hot 100 chart. She was also included in Time's annual most influential people list, known not only for her music but also for her extravagant style.

Rihanna's Music Career

Rihanna earned fame with her music. She idolized Madonna and said she wanted to be that kind of an icon for black girls. The Barbadian singer has written a lot of music that has been a big hit. Her single with Drake, Work, topped Billboard's charts.

RiRi also wrote Needed Me, Love On The Brain, and This Is What You Came For, which became big catchy hits. Although she hasn't had professional training, Rihanna has learned some things from Ne-Yo. He would tell her how to breathe while singing, and she would follow.

Her Love the Way You Lie with Eminem was absolutely loved by fans. Another of her greatest collaborations include Can't Remember To Forget You with Shakira, Famous with Kanye West, and Wild Thoughts with DJ Khaled, transformed her into a music idol. Her soprano voice is truly admired by many and inspires a lot of young artists.

What Is Rihanna's Main Source of Income?

Believe it or not, it's actually Rihanna's makeup that does better than her music. Of course, her music caught people's attention, but what made many admirers into devoted fans was her range of cosmetics.

Her cosmetic line is based on her last name and is called Fenty Beauty. It is known to be crafted with a focus on skincare and not purely chemical makeup.

She launched the fashion brand in 2019 and became the first woman of color to achieve this. Fenty Beauty sells foundations, highlighters, bronzers, blush compacts, lip glosses, and blotting sheets. The range was welcomed with open arms by the public because of its inclusivity. It has become one of the pioneers in the beauty industry that make products that are compatible with darker skin tones. Girls of color, after generations of feeling ignored by the cosmetic industry, finally felt heard and seen.

What Is Nicki Minaj's Main Source of Income?

Outside of music, Minaj'sMinaj's film career has included supporting roles in the comedy films The Other Woman in 2014 and Barbershop: The Next Cut in 2016. She also appeared as a judge on the 12th season of American Idol in 2013.

Nicki Minaj Vs. Rihanna: Who Has A Higher Net Worth?

So, how much exactly is Nicki's total net worth? According to Celebrity Net Worth, she has a net worth of $85 million, which is $10 million more than was reported in 2018. Nevertheless, her total earnings are even higher: a reported $156 million.

Rihanna's net worth is about $600 million. Still, in her early 30s, RiRi is the highest-paid singer and the richest self-made woman. However, this number was only calculated using her stock prices from September 2020. Rihanna has other mediums of income. She has a Savage X Fenty lingerie line and has also partnered with other entrepreneurs like Jessica Alba, Kylie Jenner, and Gwyneth Paltrow to join their products.

Rihanna has worked with Armani and also presented her own fashion collection at London Fashion Week. She still owns 49.99% of her different businesses. This is something that celebrities that are part of multiple industries struggle to do. Nonetheless, Rihanna maintained her determination about being the major influence on the brand image and identity as one that uplifts and empowers.

Although Rihanna is the wealthiest female musician, Nicki keeps being one of the most iconic hip-hop figures. Both artists are great icons of this generation.
