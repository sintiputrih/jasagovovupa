Who Is Bachelor 26's Kara Gandy? Her Bio, Family, Height, Job, Age

Meet Kara Gandy, Bachelor 26 contestant hailing from her hometown of Ohio.

Here you’ll learn about Kara’s parents, siblings, her job, her dancing career, and her career in fashion. Furthermore, we’ll also discuss her current relationship status. So, scroll down this Kara Gandy Bio to learn more about her.

Kara Gandy On Bachelor 26

The very first thing you should know about Kara Gandy is that she is no stranger to cameras. She stepped into the entertainment industry as As a dancer and performer. So you know she has experience working in front of the camera. Now, she is ready to find love has joined season 26 of ‘The Bachelor’.

Bachelor 26 is the season of Clayton Echard.

If you don’t know who Clayton is, you should know that he first appeared on Bachelor Nation during Michelle Young’s season. In July 2016, he worked as a sales associate for Stryker before joining the show. He began working as a Sales Representative in Columbia, Missouri, a year and a half later.

In Michelle Season’s ‘The Bachelorette,’ he failed to shine or even make an impression. So, when Clayton was announced as the new ‘bachelor,’ the fans were taken aback, and many began to wonder why he was chosen. Anyway, the show has been shot and will premiere on January 3, 2021. And Hunter, like any other contestant, deservingly deserves to be in the spotlight, regardless of whose season it is, even though she is not among Reality Steve’s top three.

Kara Gandy Age

Kara Gandy turned 30 years old in June 2021.

How Tall Is Kara Gandy?

Kara Gandy stands tall to the height of 5 feet 9 inches.

Kara Gandy Family

Very little is known about Kara Gandy’s family. She has featured her mother once on her Instagram. Based on the post, it was from the college graduation day after earning her bachelor’s degree in 2014 from the University of Cincinnati in Fashion/Apparel Design. Kara has a sister named Jenna Gandy who is available on Instagram (@jennagandy) but private at this moment.

Jenna was a Special Education Teacher in Alternative Paths Training School from May 2019 to March 2020. Before that, she worked as Behavioral Therapist at Continuum Autism Spectrum Alliance from May 2015 to November 2019. She also worked as a teacher at The Goddard School from October 2012 – May 2014.

Jenna is a graduate of Ohio University with a Bachelor’s degree in Psychology.

Furthermore, Kara also has a brother named Rance Gandy who works as Pharmacy Business Consultant at Cardinal Health. He had previously worked as Sr. Analyst, Territory Management, Inside Sales Consultant, and ParMed-Pharmaceutical Sales Representative in the same institution.

Rance attended Olentangy Liberty High School and graduated from Ohio University with a Bachelor of Science – BS, Health Service Administration.

Kara Gandy Job

Marie Claire magazine had featured her in the #beautyroadshow segment. “I like the single dimple on my right cheek. Two of my cousins share the same trait,” she told in the September 2015 issue of the magazine.

As a dancer, Kara performed on the relaunch of MTV’s Total Request Live back in 2017 with her crew. They opened up the show with DJ Khaled. In addition, she also danced with GenX at a launch party for Serena Williams’ docu-series “Being Serena” in 2018.

There are various clips of Kara dancing in Brickhouse studio in New York City.

Back in October 2020, she also danced in the music video of artist TKaiia‘s song “Good for Me“. Kara also performed with rapper Slick Rick in March 2019 for the Good Morning America show.

Apart from her career as a dancer, she is also a fashion designer. You can check out her Pinterest (@aragandy) for more info. She has worked as a designer at Madewell, worked as a Freelance Performance Designer at Calvin Klein, Designer at Aeropostale, and Associate Designer at EXPRESS.

Before that, Kara had interned at Tory Burch, Aerie by AEO, Lands’ End, Aeropostale, and Abercombie & Fitch.

According to the reports of Glassdoor, the fashion designer at Madewell makes nearly $64,387 per year.

Fun Fact: Kara has modeled for a phenomenal painting. The name of the painting is “Patina” Acrylic on Linen 24×30.
