Who Is The Ultimatum Madlyn Ballatori Dad, Luigi Ballatori?

Madlyn Ballatori from The Ultimatum is the daughter of Luigi Ballatori. Talking about her dad, Luigi was born on 12 March 1962 which makes him 60 years old. He is currently serving as the CEO of Ballatori Enterprises since September 2019.

In 1987, Luigi Ballatori created the first version of Landpro, a Lease Management Software system geared for Oil & Gas Exploration companies and the Mining Industry. This led to the official formation of LandPro Corp, which specializes in Land Management and GIS Mapping Software. Visual LandPro, the company’s flagship product, keeps track of leases, tracts, contracts, division orders, delay rentals, acreage calculations, payment obligations, special provisions, and much more.

He served as the CEO of Landpro Corp since March 1990 whereas he started working as President of the company in March 2019 before he left the company in August 2019.

Before that, Luigi was a senior programmer at Sunicor, Inc. starting from September 1987 to February 1990. He worked as Geologist at TS Brown Exploration and Revenue Accountant at Seagull International Exploration.

Luigi is a graduate of the University of Houston in 1986. He is available on Facebook (@Luigi.Ballatori) and Instagram (@woodlands_vt).

Madlyn recently featured her dad on Father’s Day 2021. She posted a short clip of her father from when she was young.

Who Is The Ultimatum Madlyn Ballatori Mom, Sheila Langley Blalock?

Madlyn Balatori’s mom Sheila Langley Blalock married her father Luigi Ballatori on 1 January 2001. They divorced on 28 July 2015.

On 12 November 1970, Sheila turned 51 years old.

Does The Ultimatum Madlyn Ballatori Have Siblings?
