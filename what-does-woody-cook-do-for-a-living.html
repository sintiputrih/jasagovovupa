What Does Woody Cook do For A Living?

Woody Cook is a model, DJ, and presenter.

Woody Cook kicked off his career by stepping into the world of modeling, earning representation from the prestigious PRM Agency. His foray into the modeling industry showcased not only his striking looks but also his potential to make a lasting impact in the entertainment world.

Woody gained widespread recognition as a contestant on the 2019 season of “The Circle.” The reality television series, known for its unique social media-themed format, provided Woody with a platform to showcase his authentic self. During his time on the show, Woody’s openness and honesty resonated with viewers. Notably, he used his platform to discuss and reveal his bisexuality, contributing to important conversations about representation and acceptance.

Woody Cook’s journey extends beyond the confines of reality television. With a keen eye for fashion and a charismatic presence, he continues to explore diverse opportunities within the entertainment and modeling spheres. By openly discussing his bisexuality on a public platform like “The Circle,” Woody contributes to the ongoing dialogue surrounding LGBTQ+ visibility and acceptance.
