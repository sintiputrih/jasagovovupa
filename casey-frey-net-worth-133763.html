Casey Frey Net Worth

Casey Frey is an American comedian and social media star born on June 23, 1993. He is best known for his comedic videos on Vine, where he amassed over 250,000 followers before the platform shut down. He also has a large following on Instagram, with over 60,000 followers, where he continues to post comedic videos. In addition to his social media career, Frey has experience as a dancer and theater actor.
