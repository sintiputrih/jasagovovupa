Egypt Sherrod Kids With Her Husband DJ Mike Jackson, Family

Fans love the chemistry between Egypt Sherrod and her husband, DJ Mike Jackson.

In addition, they are curious to know about Egypt Sherrod Kids and their off-screen lives.

Born on November 16, 1976, Egypt Sherrod is an American real estate broker as well as a radio and television personality. She was born in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania.

Sherrod received a Bachelor of Arts degree in broadcasting and telecommunications from Temple University.

Moreover, she went to New York University’s Tisch School of the Arts to study theater.

Egypt is a certified real estate expert and holds the designation of Master Certified Negotiator, Certified Distressed Property Expert, Accredited Buyer Representative, and Certified Residential Specialist.

How Many Kids Does Egypt Sherrod have?

Mike Jackson and Egypt Sherrod have been happily married for 13 years.

They tied the knot on September 11, 2010, at the West Mount Country Club in New Jersey.

The couple is proud parents of two beautiful daughters: Kendall and Harper.

However, Egypt was focused on her career and didn’t plan to have kids. But she got pregnant in 2011.

Kendall was born in January 2012. Soon after her birth, they moved to Atlanta as Egypt was hired at the WVEE radio station in the city.

After their firstborn, they decided to add a new member to the family, so they planned on having a second child.

But the medical condition of Egypt made it difficult for the couple. Egypt was suffering from ‘Chronic Endometriosis.’

The doctor suggested a partial hysterectomy, but the pair were determined to have another child.

The next thing led to another, Egypt and Jackson brought their second daughter, Harper, into the world in February 2019 despite a high risk of post-partum complications.

Now, they are a happy family of four. The powerful duo never misses a chance to share pictures on their social media with their adorable daughters.

They keep updating about their family life to fans.

Egypt Sherrod Husband DJ Mike Jackson

Mike Jackson is a professional DJ. He goes by the name DJ Fadelf.

Apart from being a DJ, he is also a realtor, licensed contractor, fitness trainer, and author.

He is a supportive husband of Egypt Sherrod and loves to work with her.

Oftentimes, the power couple is seen together helping each other on renovation projects.

Additionally, they have appeared together on shows like ‘Rock the Block, ‘Black Love,’ and ‘Married to Real Estate.’

However, their relationship was not always the same. They had to go through some rollercoaster journey before making it official.

Mike instantly fell in love with Egypt after seeing her at an event in New York. But it took him a lot of time and perseverance to convince her.

He approached her several times, but she rejected him.

Sherrod was skeptical of dating him because of his conceited personality and link to the music industry.

But they were fated to be together. One time Egypt asked her mentor for references to assist her in a project house-flipping. To her surprise, Mike was the chosen one.

After working closely with Jackson, she was swooned over by his hard work and dedication. 

Eventually, the couple started dating in 2004 and got engaged in 2008. They decided to get hitched.

But once more, Sherrod had second thoughts about it because of DJ’s past relationship.

Mike was previously married to a mysterious woman and had a daughter named Simone Nikolej. He was having conflict with his ex-wife over his daughter, Simone. 

This made Egypt insecure, and she decided to postpone their marriage.

But they got past it and eventually exchanged vows on September 11, 2010.

How Much is Egypt Sherrod Net Worth?

According to Celebrity Networth, the estimated net worth of Egypt Sherrod is $6 million approximately.

Sherrod’s primary source of income is her successful career as a real estate expert and host for WVEE, a renowned radio station based in Atlanta, Georgia.

Furthermore, she serves as the host and executive producer of HGTV’s “Property Virgins.”

Also, Sherrod is a great speaker, and moderator who has hosted the stage at Oprah’s “The Life You Want Tour.”

Similarly, she earns from numerous shows. She has appeared in shows like “The Maury Show”, “Home Delivery”, and “The Black Carpet.”

Egypt has been honored with numerous accolades for her broadcast and entrepreneurial work.

She has also been featured in various publications like Time Out New York, Source Magazine, Black Enterprise, and Today’s Black Woman.
