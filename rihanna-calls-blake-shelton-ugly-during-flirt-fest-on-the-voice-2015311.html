Rihanna Calls Blake Shelton Ugly During Flirt Fest on The Voice

Some friendly — or flirty — joking. Rihanna and Blake Shelton engaged in some heavy flirting on the Monday, Nov. 2, episode of The Voice, but it didn’t go so well for the country hunk.

It started innocently enough, when the Voice coach, 39, and the pop star, 27, bantered over nicknames for each other.

“I think you should reconsider my nickname for you, ‘Anna,’” the country singer told the Barbadian songstress. “Or do you like RiRi?”

“RiRi is not new!” the “Umbrella” singer shot back. “You thought you were so brilliant!”

Miranda Lambert’s ex-husband coyly replied, “I thought of that nickname years ago, because I am a fan.”

The two both praised a pretty female contestant on the country crooner’s Team Blake, and as she walked away after advancing, Shelton cracked that “she should work on being cuter.”
