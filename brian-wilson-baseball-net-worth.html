Brian Wilson (baseball) Net Worth

Brian Wilson Net Worth: $16 Million

Brian Wilson, the American professional baseball player, has a net worth of $16 million. He is best known for his cutter, slider, two-seam fastball, and four-seam fastball. He played with the San Francisco Giants from 2006 until 2012 before joining the Los Angeles Dodgers in 2013. Wilson won one World Series championship in his career in 2010 as a member of the Giants. He is a three-time MLB All-Star, earning the honors in 2008, 2010, and 2011. Wilson’s giant black beard has attracted quite a bit of media attention, appearing on several shows, including “The Tonight Show with Jay Lenzo” and a handful of commercials. During his carer, Brian Wilson earned $40 million in salary alone.
