WWE Pitched Idea For Bray Wyatt &amp; The Undertaker For RAW 30th Anniversary Show

WWE is celebrating RAW’s 30th anniversary this week, and there are plenty of big names expected for the show. Hulk Hogan, Shawn Michaels, Booker T and more will be there. The Undertaker is also slated to attend, but his wife Michelle McCool wasn’t called. That being said, WWE might have some big plans for Taker.

Bray Wyatt and The Undertaker met in the ring before. Since WWE is going into Wyatt’s history, Taker’s return for one night only could provide WWE with a golden opportunity.

Sean Sapp reported a very interesting note behind Fightful’s paywall. While it was not confirmed that this will happen, there were pitches within WWE for Bray Wyatt and The Undertaker to have some kind of segment togther this week.

There were pitches for Undertaker to be involved with a Bray Wyatt/LA Knight segment. 

It was also noted that, “Despite his look on the poster, The Undertaker’s traditional gear was sent to the show.” So, the Deadman could show up for the 30th anniversary of RAW.

The 30th anniversary of RAW will also serve as the go-home episode before the 2023 Royal Rumble event. WWE has a lot of loose ends to tie up, and you never know what can happen before Bray Wyatt and LA Knight meet in the Mountain Dew Pitch Black match in the Alamodome.

There will likely be more news breaking as we approach the big show. Keep checking back with Ringside News as we continue monitoring this ongoing situation.
