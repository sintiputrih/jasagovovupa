Hailey Baldwin Wedding Dress Details

Hailey Baldwin and Justin Bieber have finally tied the knot, over a year after first visiting a marriage license courthouse n New York City. The couple became Mr. and Mrs. in a religious ceremony in South Carolina; and although the event was top-secret, fans are likely already scrambling for whatever intel from the festivities they can get. One of the most sought-after details? The bride's gown.

Although photos have not yet gone public from the ceremony, here's everything we know about Hailey Baldwin's wedding dress so far.

She had a designer chosen a year ago.

Speaking to The Cut, in an article published in September 2018, Baldwin revealed that while her wedding dress has not yet been made, she has selected a designer for it. Basically, the wedding planning is in full swing.

But she's remained tight-lipped on who the designer is.

According to E!, Baldwin attended the Carolina Herrera show at New York Fashion Week, where she sat with model Lily Aldridge. Speaking to an E! News reporter, Aldridge apparently teased, "I'm the designer," with Baldwin jumping in to say, "She's actually designing it for me."

Meanwhile, Cosmopolitan speculates that Virgil Abloh could be in the running to design Baldwin's gown:

She already teased the shade of the dress.

Offering the vaguest description possible, Hailey said in a video that her gown "kind of like an off white color." We'll take what we can get.

Baldwin has already worn so many potential wedding gowns.

On the red carpet, the model has often worn dresses that wouldn't be out of place at a wedding. Take this gown by Roberto Cavalli, for instance:

Then there was the Tommy Hilfiger gown she wore to the 2018 Met Gala, which she attended with Shawn Mendes:

And there was the Carolina Herrera skirt and crop top that Baldwin wore to the 2017 Met Gala, which gave off serious bridal vibes:

She went the affordable route for her bachelorette party.

For her "wild" pre-wedding fête (organized by pal Kendall Jenner, no less), Hailey opted for a white, strapless number by Oh Polly covered in ruched detailing. She wore a veil and Amina Muaddi pumps with the body-hugging dress, as she and her friends sauntered around West Hollywood.

She wore custom Vivienne Westwood for the rehearsal dinner.

The night before the wedding, Hailey dressed up in a romantic, off-the-shoulder mini dress custom-made by Westwood at the designer's London atelier. In a statement, Westwood explained that the bride's gown was "made with organically-produced, eco-friendly and cruelty-free peace silk — which allows the silk butterfly to live beyond the cocoon," per People. She paired the number with a bow in her hair and bow-accented white sandals.

For now, at least, we'll have to continue dreaming about what the model actually wore on her big day.
