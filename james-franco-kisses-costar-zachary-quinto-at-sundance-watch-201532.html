James Franco Kisses Costar Zachary Quinto at Sundance: Watch

And there was a popcorn shower! James Franco and Zachary Quinto took advantage of The New York Times’ sponsored slo-mo video booth at the 2015 Sundance Film Festival. The costars, who get it on together in upcoming flick I Am Michael, shared an extremely slow, drawn-out kiss in the video booth.

It all starts with Franco and Quinto staring into each other’s eyes and leaning in close to one another. After a series of other actors are shown doing various movements in slow motion, the clip then ends with the duo finally locking lips underneath a popcorn shower before erupting into laughter.

I am Michael is based on a New York Times article written by Benoit Denizet-Lewis titled “My Ex-Gay Friend.” The flick tells the story of a former gay activist who becomes an anti-gay Christian fundamentalist after announcing he’s straight.
