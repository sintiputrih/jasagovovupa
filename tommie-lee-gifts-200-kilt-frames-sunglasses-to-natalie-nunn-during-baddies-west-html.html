Tommie Lee gifts $200 Kilt Frames sunglasses to Natalie Nunn during Baddies West

Tommie Lee, known for her fame from Love and Hip Hop Atlanta and her role in the third season of Baddies West on the Zeus Network, recently surprised her co-star Natalie Nunn with a special gift during an episode in February 202Let’s take a closer look at Tommie Lee’s sunglasses range, which she graciously presented to Natalie.

Meet Tommie from Baddies West:

Tommie Lee is a part of the 2023 cast of Baddies West, which premiered on The Zeus Network on January 2This reality show features a star-studded lineup, including Natalie Nunn, Chrisean Rock, Biggie, Rollie, Razor, and Stunna Girl. Tommie, who first gained recognition through Love and Hip Hop Atlanta, also took on the role of a judge during the Baddies West Auditions alongside Natalie Nunn and Sukihana from Love and Hip Hop Miami.

Tommie Lee’s Sunglasses Surprise:

During an episode of Baddies West in 2023, Tommie Lee decided to share a thoughtful gift with her friend and co-star, Natalie Nunn. She gifted Natalie a pair of her ‘Night Out’ Kilt Frames sunglasses. This heartwarming moment was also shared on Tommie’s TikTok page, ensuring that fans who missed it got to witness the touching gesture.

The Stylish ‘Night Out’ Kilt Frames:

Tommie Lee’s sunglasses collection, known as ‘Night Out’ Kilt Frames, has been making waves. These trendy shades come with a unique feature – built-in Bluetooth technology. So not only do they make a fashion statement, but they also offer a convenient and stylish way to stay connected. If you want to grab a pair of these chic sunglasses, they are available for around $200, according to the Kilt Frames website. Tommie first teased the design of these sunglasses on her Instagram in 2016, promising that they would be “coming soon.” Well, in 2023, they are officially up for sale, and fans can’t get enough of them.

Tommie Lee’s Net Worth in 2024:

Aside from her reality TV fame, Tommie Lee has ventured into entrepreneurship with her Kilt Frames business. She has also dabbled in music and maintains a YouTube channel with over 130k subscribers. As of 2023, Full Celebs reports Tommie’s estimated net worth at $300,000, while Wealthy Genius places it at $200,000. It’s worth noting that in 2018, Tommie was reported to be earning up to $17,000 per season, according to Love and Hip Hop Wiki.

Conclusion:

Tommie Lee’s kind gesture of gifting Natalie Nunn a pair of her ‘Night Out’ Kilt Frames sunglasses during a Baddies West episode has warmed the hearts of fans. This reality TV star turned entrepreneur continues to make a name for herself, not only through her on-screen appearances but also with her stylish and tech-savvy eyewear collection. As her net worth continues to grow, Tommie Lee’s influence in the entertainment industry remains undeniable, making her a force to be reckoned with in 2023.
