Watch: Money Heist Season 4 Trailer

Netflix has released the first trailer of the 4th season of Money Heist.

Money Heist is the most popular non-English language show on Netflix.

The end of Season 3 saw The Professor on the run and other scenes proved that Raquel is still alive.

Season 4’s trailer begins with Nairobi on a stretcher and The Professor running — or should we say escaping through a forest.

Money Heist Season 4 hits your screens on the 4th of April, 2020.
