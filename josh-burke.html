Biography

Josh Burke was born in Syracuse, NY on October 5, 1993. Best recognized as the lead guitarist for the Florida-born punk band The Red Jumpsuit Apparatus. Joining the band in 2011, he has since worked on their EP Et Tu, Brute and the album simply titled 4. He is joined in The Red Jumpsuit Apparatus by brothers Ronnie and Randy Winter, both singers and guitarists. Having been a fan of The Red Jumpsuit Apparatus for many years previously, after former guitarists Duke Kitchens and Matt Carter stepped down, he became the lead guitarist for the band in July of 2011.
