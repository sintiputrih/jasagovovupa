Westport Police Charge Sandy Hook Woman With Harrassing Attorney

Katherine Sullivan, 50, was charged with second-degree harassment. She posted bond and will appear in court April 20.

Westport police responded to the Newtown Police Department on Saturday, where Sullivan was being held on an outstanding warrant issued by Westport police. The arrest stems from a harassment complaint made by a local attorney who previously represented Sullivan, police said.

Sullivan was ordered by a Westport officer not to have any further contact with the attorney, but police said that she continued to harass the attorney. This resulted in an arrest warrant being issued.
