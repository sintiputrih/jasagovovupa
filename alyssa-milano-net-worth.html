What is Alyssa Milano’s Net Worth and Salary?

Alyssa Milano, the American actress, producer and singer, has a net worth of $10 million dollars. She gained fame in the 1980s for her role in the sitcom “Who’s the Boss?”. Milano has also starred in television movies and series such as “Charmed”. Additionally, she appeared in the short-lived sitcom “Romantically Challenged” and the film “Hall Pass”.

Alyssa Milano’s Background

Alyssa Jayne Milano was born on December 19, 1972, in Brooklyn, New York City. She was raised Roman Catholic in a family of Italian descent. Her parents are Lin Milano, a fashion designer and talent manager, and Thomas M. Milano, a film-music editor. She has a younger brother named Cory, who is ten years younger than her.

Alyssa Milano’s Early Career

At the age of 7, Alyssa Milano’s babysitter took her to an audition for “Annie” without her parents’ permission. Milano beat out over 1,500 other girls and was chosen for one of the four principal parts. She then traveled with the touring company for 18 months alongside her mother. Upon their return to New York, Milano appeared in various television commercials and off-Broadway productions.

Alyssa Milano’s Career Highlights

Alyssa Milano made her film debut in “Old Enough” in 1984, and shortly after landed the role of Tony Danza’s daughter on the hit sitcom “Who’s the Boss?”. She starred on the show for 8 seasons, becoming a household name and teen idol. She also starred in films like “Commando” and on stage in productions like “Butterflies Are Free”.

Milano was offered a five-album record deal, and released four studio albums that she has been openly critical of. She continued to work on various projects, including producing a teen workout video “Teen Steam” and starring in films like “Where the Day Takes You”.

In an attempt to distance herself from her “nice girl” image, Milano appeared in several erotic films like “Embrace of the Vampire”. She later starred in seasons 5-7 of “Melrose Place” and in all 8 seasons of “Charmed”, where she also became a producer. She has appeared on shows like “Mistresses” and “Insatiable”, and in films like “My Girlfriend’s Boyfriend” and “Hall Pass”.

Alyssa Milano’s Earnings on “Charmed”

Alyssa Milano earned $90,000 per episode at the peak of her time on “Charmed”. This equates to approximately $2 million per season.

Relationships and Family

Alyssa Milano had a relationship with actor Corey Haim from 1987 to 1990, during which he struggled with addiction. Milano and his manager attempted to get him professional help, but were unsuccessful. In 1993, she became engaged to actor Scott Wolf but they ended their engagement and separated a year later.

In 2019, Milano revealed that she had gotten pregnant with Wolf while on birth control, and underwent two abortions in 1993 while they were still together.

In 2008, Milano got engaged to David Bugliari, an agent at Creative Artists Agency, after a year of dating. They got married in August 2009 at Bugliari’s family home. They have two children together, a son born in August 2011, and a daughter born in September 2014.

Alyssa Milano’s Financial Woes

In 2017, Alyssa Milano sued her former business manager for leaving her millions of dollars in debt and ruining her credit. She also sued her accountant for allowing the costs of a home renovation to soar to $5 million, despite the fact that the home was only worth $3 million after the renovations. Milano was under the impression that her finances were in order and turned down lucrative acting roles, but in reality, she and her husband were millions of dollars in debt. The accountant reportedly failed to make payments to the IRS, leading to millions of dollars in penalties and unpaid taxes.

Furthermore, Milano alleged that the accountant forged her name to make bad investments, such as a parking lot. In the aftermath of these financial issues, Milano put her $2 million West Hollywood condo up for sale.
