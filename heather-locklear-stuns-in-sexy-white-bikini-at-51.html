Heather Locklear Rocks White Bikini at 51

Kate Upton's not the only blond bombshell to wow in a white two-piece this summer.

While on vacation in Hawaii, Heather Locklear opted for a strappy ivory bikini top with crochet skirt bottom.

The former Melrose Place star, 51, accessorized her bathing suit with a straw fedora, blue jewelry and dark shades. Outfit aside, it was her fit figure that was truly head-turning. Flaunting a flat belly and toned arms the star looked completely carefree—and yes, ageless as always!—as she laughed with friends while strolling on the beach.
