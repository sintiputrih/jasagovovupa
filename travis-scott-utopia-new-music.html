Travis Scott is Dropping New Music from Upcoming Album 'Utopia'

Following the announcement of his highly anticipated festival Astroworld, as well as the confirmation of a new Nike collaboration release, Travis Scott is also releasing new music tonight.

Revealed by the rapper himself on Instagram, the release comes as a surprise to many. "FUCK IT LETS START IT UP, NEW MUSIC AT MIDNIGHT," he wrote, accompanied by a black-and-white poster of himself photoshopped into a cartoonish creature.

It remains to be seen what is set to drop. According to previous teasers, his upcoming album is dubbed Utopia, and we know for a fact that Scott has been working alongside A24 to create something bigger and better around his fourth studio album.

Elsewhere, Cactus Jack has been busy this year, teaming up with Nike and fragment for an Air Jordan 1 collaboration, as well as continuing his partnership with the Swoosh at Astroworld. The rapper was also featured on Kanye West's DONDA as well as on Drake's Certified Lover Boy and is also expecting another child with Kylie Jenner. He also teamed up with Kim Jones and collaborated with Dior on a capsule collection earlier this year, but none of it made fans forget about his rumored album.

Although we probably won't get the full album just yet, whatever is dropping will definitely be part of the upcoming record.

Stay tuned as any more information surfaces.
