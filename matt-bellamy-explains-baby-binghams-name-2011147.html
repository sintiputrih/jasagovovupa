Matt Bellamy Explains Baby Bingham's Name

Bing bing bing!

Kate Hudson and Matt Bellamy didn't just name their newborn son Bingham because they liked the name; it has major family significance!

"For those wondering, Bingham is my mum's maiden name," the Muse frontman, 33, tweeted Thursday afternoon of his mother Marilyn. "Bing Russell was also Kurt [Russell's] dad. Family connections all around!"

Although the actress' biological father is Bill Hudson, she considers Russell, 60, to be her father. (The actor has been Goldie Hawn's partner for 28 years, although they have never wed.) The tot's full name — Bingham "Bing" Hawn — also pays homage to Hudson's mom, 65.

Bing was born Saturday night in L.A. after "4.5 hours of intense pushing," according to Bellamy. "Mum and baby are strong and healthy," he added.
